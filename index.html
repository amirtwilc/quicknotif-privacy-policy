Privacy Policy for Quick Notif

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Quick Notif ("the App") is developed and published by Amir Twil-Cohen ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

2. Data Collection

Quick Notif does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

3. Local Data Storage

All notifications created within the App are stored locally on the user's device only.

  • Notification titles and scheduling data remain on the device.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server.
  • No cloud storage is used.

If the App is uninstalled, all stored data is permanently deleted from the device.

4. Permissions Used

Quick Notif may request the following permissions:

Notification Permission

Used solely to display notifications created by the user.

Exact Alarm Permission (if applicable)

Used to schedule notifications at specific times selected by the user.

These permissions are used strictly for the App’s core functionality and for no other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

Quick Notif does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

6. Children's Privacy

Quick Notif is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. However, since the App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Security

Because Quick Notif does not transmit or collect personal data, all information remains stored locally on the user’s device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected by modifying the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
